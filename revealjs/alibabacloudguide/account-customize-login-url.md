You can customize the login URL by specifying a company alias. To do
this:

-   Go to the RAM console,

-   Click on Settings,

-   Click on Advanced,

-   Click on Edit next to Default Domain,

-   Enter the new domain (in the form XXX.onaliyun.com),

-   Click on OK.
